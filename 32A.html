ECS 32A Tutoring

Welcome to CS Peer Tutoring! Every quarter, the CS Tutoring Committee organizes undergraduate volunteer tutors to help students with undergraduate CS courses. All tutors are approved by the CS department, and received high grades in the courses they tutor.

On this page, you can find the hours for all of our ECS 32A tutors this quarter. We show tutoring hours for the next 7 days. If this page is not loading correctly, you can always see the full list of tutors here (which lists tutors for all courses).

CS tutoring is free for all students --- just head down to Kemper 75 (in the basement of Kemper Hall, look for signs pointing to the CSIF) at one of the times shown below and sign in on the whiteboard.

Please note that tutors may show up a few minutes after the listed time here (eg. if they have a class beforehand). Contact ucdcstutoring@gmail.com with any questions.
